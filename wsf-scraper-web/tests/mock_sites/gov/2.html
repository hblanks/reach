UK NCP Steering Board meeting minutes: 17 October 2018

Minutes from the Steering Board for the UK NCP for the OECD’s guidelines for multinational enterprises.

Published 6 February 2019
From:
Department for International Trade

Documents

UK NCP Steering Board meeting minutes: 17 October 2018

PDF, 120KB, 4 pages

Details

The work of the UK National Contact Point (UK NCP) for the OECD’s guidelines for multinational enterprises is overseen by a steering board.

To ensure that the board reflects a wide range of views, its members come from:

  • business
  • trade unions
  • non-governmental organisations
  • other government departments

Find out more about the UK NCP Steering Board.
